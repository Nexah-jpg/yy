Tasks Instruction and Guidance

Here are the instructions of each task:

Mini-task #1

The task was all about making a visual representation out of the given statement "I love Chicken" by using any kind of an editing software.This is the easiest task among the other tasks distributed since representing something on your own understanding or perspective isn't hard. The more effort you exert, the more creative you will be.

Mini-task #2

In this task, we were expected to select a still or steady photo of ourselves and make different scenes out of it by using any kind of editing software. This task was a little bit hard to do especially for the first-timers who weren't able to experience this kind of activity.(Sorry, Attachment not available due to technical problems.)

Mini-task #3

This task is all about Photography. We have to take a photo of ourselves in one of our favorite area at home and then use any kind of editing software to make our photo even more impressive. In this generation, People who pose effortlessly and still looks attractive in photographs are called photogenic. It would be unchallenging for them to do this task.

Mini-task #4

This task starts to be a little bit of challenging because you have to be creative in doing it. It is all about advertising your product or making a PRINT-AD using any available editing software. Advertising your product in a significant and an eye-catching way would be a great help with the business you're working on. Opportunities and negotiations will rise when you advertise your product effectively.

Mini-task #5

It still talks about advertising, but this is different from the recent task you have read. In this task, you have to make a Point-of-Purchase(POP) Material for your product. POP Materials are marketing tools used for advertising your products or it could be merchandising. POP Materials could be standees and banners that you can see in the market or the billboards that you have seen on the street, or many other ways of advertising.Big companies Advertise their products on televisions, social medias, and other websites since it is more effective.

Mini-task #6

It is still related with Mini-task #5 information but in this task you will be doing a Point-of-Purchase(POP) Display for your product. POP displays are also marketing materials placed beside the merchandise you're promoting. Shelves are one of the examples of POP Displays.
