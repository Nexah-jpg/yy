Yamanan comes from the tagalog word "Kayamanan" which means "treasure" in the english language. We decided to use Yamanan as a name for our project since it is a very rare name and we believe that it will thrive soon. This is all about helping each other in building success for the future and training oneself to become a competent professional. In fact, anyone who wants to attain his/her profession must experience great hardwork and practice. The more skillful a person is, the more opportunities will come. Big companies commonly hire high-skilled workers for performance improvement and production boost which helps the business grow even more than what they have expected. We encourage people to turn his or her expectations into reality where they can be what they want to be and to also improve oneself performance and deeds.


Yamanan is for young entrepreneurs or small business establishments where they can learn how to advertise, promote, and as well as negotiate. We aim to help start up businesses expand their company and creat on their own. This website is not popular yet the reason for making this project is to help wanna-be entrepreneurs and advertisers to become what they want to be. In this content, you are freely able to showcase your designs and ideas with no limit and to discover different kinds of designs made by other users. If you like to start something, it is best to understand and acquire the foundation first before doing anything. Create a straight plan, set a goal, negotiate, invest, and build up. These are the things you should consider before starting something big. Think like your planting a seed and that it will soon grow in the right time and develop into something convenient for yourself, for your community, or rather for your country.
